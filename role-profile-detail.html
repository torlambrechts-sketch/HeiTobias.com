Trait target bands

Ranges, not maxima

Personality and integrity targets, facet-level where evidence supports. Each band has a direction; a bare maximum is architecturally disallowed (PHASE0-SPEC §2.7 C3). Bands default to optimum for Conscientiousness & Emotional Stability per the inverted-U literature. Le, Oh, Robbins, Ilies, Holland, Westrick (2011) JAP; Pierce & Aguinis (2013) TMGT; Grant (2013) ambivert effect; Oh, Wang, Mount (2011) HEXACO Honesty-Humility.

Conscientiousness IPIP-NEO · 12 facets · 120-item
Optimum band target 60–80 (centre 70) · weight 0.18

Why a band, not "more is better." Above ~80 (T-score), conscientiousness becomes counterproductive for engineering work — over-checking, perfectionism on low-stakes paths, difficulty with the structured-improvisation incidents demand. Below 60, missed commitments and inconsistent quality dominate. Evidence: Le et al. (2011) inverted-U β = −0.06 quadratic; Pierce & Aguinis (2013) too-much-of-a-good-thing meta-analysis. Facet emphasis: Achievement-Striving, Self-Discipline, Order; de-emphasize Deliberation in incident-response sub-roles.

Optimum band target 55–80 (centre 68) · weight 0.14

On-call rotation activates this trait (Trait Activation Theory, §6). The role's high adversity-exposure context makes the lower bound matter — below 55, stress response interferes with incident judgement. Above 80, the same insensitivity that helps in incidents can dampen useful concern about edge-case risks. Evidence: Le et al. (2011); Tett & Burnett (2003) TAT — adversity is the activating cue.

Values & motivation

Schwartz value priorities + Self-Determination Theory needs-supplies fit (autonomy / competence / relatedness). Operationalized as wished-for value alignment for the role context, not a personality test. Schwartz (1992, 2012); Deci & Ryan (2000); Kristof-Brown values congruence.

Schwartz value priorities — role-fit weighting

Self-direction Achievement Stimulation Universalism Benevolence Power Tradition Conformity
Roles for which Self-direction and Achievement dominate suit candidates whose Schwartz portrait points the same way. Power and Conformity dominance is a misalignment signal for this role — not a candidate flaw.

Evolution vector

How the role is expected to change over the next 12–18 months. This is a forecast, not a measurement. Sourced, confidence-rated, with a forced re-review date. Used to inform development conversations and re-fit signalling — never to grade a person. SCIENCE-SPEC §5 "Evolution vector"; §6 fit-as-trajectory.

Forecast — not a measurement Confidence: Medium · Horizon: 18 months · Next review due: 22 Nov 2026

The role will absorb more open-ended architectural ambiguity as the platform migrates from monolith-with-services to a service-mesh. Influence-through-writing (RFCs) and cross-team coordination will weigh more; pure individual delivery will weigh less. Cognitive complexity ticks up from 4 toward 5; ambiguity-tolerance context factor moves from 4 to ~4.5.
